Who We Are

Welcome, and thank you for visiting the Bloomfield Republican Town Committee website.

We meet on the third Tuesday evening of every month at 6 p.m. in Bloomfield’s Town Hall Council Chambers.

Attending our meetings will offer you a great way to meet like-minded residents and to know what is happening in town. Our Republican Town Council members fill us in on Council happenings and elected and appointed members of other town offices and town committees including the Board of Education, report on the activities they’re involved in. You’ll be updated on topics such as zoning, energy, ethics, education, and more.

Bloomfield Republicans believe in government being fiscally responsible, responsive to the needs of its citizens, but allowing each citizen to grow, prosper, and enjoy life in his or her own individual way.

The Republicans have proven this by advocating low taxes and providing services our neighbors have come to expect and require.

Our Republican elected officials work hard at keeping our Town a small Town-with great benefits. We believe we are neighbors. We look out for one another and demand that elected officials spend the taxpayer’s money wisely.

You can find us on Facebook under BloomfieldCtGOP. If you would like to receive updates from us or find out more information, email us at secretary@bloomfieldctgop.com. We look forward to hearing from you, and hope to see you at one of our next meetings.
